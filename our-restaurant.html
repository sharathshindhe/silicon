The Guest

Guests can Meet, Greet, Eat and Sleep All in One Place

What matters most to Clarion guests is what happens when they interact together with business associates, partners, family and friends. The typical Clarion guest, who is between 35 and 60, active, married and employed full-time, is searching for a suitable venue for an event. Clarion guests value the social side of getting together and connecting with people. Some gather for business — like a conference or trade show — and others for leisure — like a high school reunion or bridal shower. But whatever the event, they want it to be stress-free and enjoyable. That’s why they look for full-service facilities, with attentive services and an appealing ambience, as well as ample meeting space, catering and a casual restaurant, social bar or lounge.
