Rooms and Suites

Guests can Meet, Greet, Eat and Sleep All in One Place

Being in the middle is a great place to be — and that’s exactly where Clarion is. We are the middle ground between bigger hotels focused on booking large-scale events at expensive rates, and smaller hotels with limited services and space. Clarion has the mid-scale hotel space and is focused on attracting, serving and satisfying small to medium-size social and business events. By providing convenient facilities, attentive service and accessible value, we’re the one-stop solution to planning successful events, meetings and gatherings.

Deluxe Room

Spacious contemporary rooms are Wi-Fi accessible, and offer a flat-screen TV, a personal safe, and a wardrobe. Coffee/tea making facilities and a minibar are included and an iron is available on request. The en suite bathroom comes with either bath or shower facilities.
